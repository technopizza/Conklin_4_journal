What are five different java data types? What arethey used for?

1. int (positive or negative whole numbers, used for counting)
2. boolean  (true or false, used in logic operators and conditional tasks)
3. char (single ASCII character, used when manipulating text)
4. long (whole numbers up to 2^63 - 1)
5. float  (numbers with a decimal point, used when manipulating non-wholenumbers )
