
Why, then, are students encouraged to learn CS?
	Students are encouraged to learn CS because it helps those students to practice higher-level cognitive function of infering and creating new ideas based on given information, as opposed to merely regurgitating information.
	Learning the rules and patterns of CS and how they apply across different languages and situations is a model for real life success
What should students learn from CS?
	Students should be learning how to solve problems and extrapolate solutions given limited information
