Describe- Why did Steve Ballmer call the GPL a virus?
	Steve Balmer claims that the open-source nature of the GPL cause all of a company's software products to be affected. Therefore public companies, such as Microsoft, cannot use the GPL.
What is the relationship / difference between open source and proprietary software?
	Proprietary software is owned by a corporate entity and access toits source code is restricted. Open Source, however is freely distributable and source code is available to anyone.
What are the differences between the GPL and the MIT license? 
	GPL lisenced software must be made open source when distributed. MIT lisenced software that is modified and distributed need not be made open source.
